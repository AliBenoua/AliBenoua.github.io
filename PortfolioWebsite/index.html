01 — About

A bit about me

I'm an Artificial Intelligence Master's student at the University of Sheffield, having graduated with a First-class BSc in Computer Science from the University of Liverpool. My focus is on machine learning at scale — training and tuning models for computer vision, natural language processing and beyond.

In summer 2025 I interned at Google DeepMind, where I developed a novel YOLO architecture for liquid instance segmentation in robotic chemistry, reaching 97% accuracy.
